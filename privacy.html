Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 2026

Studio Style Pro ("we", "our", or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and share information when you visit our website and sign up for our waiting list.

1. Information We Collect

When you join our waiting list, we collect:

  • Your name
  • Your email address

2. How We Use Your Information

We use your information solely to:

  • Notify you about the launch of Studio Style Pro.
  • Send you occasional updates regarding development progress or early access invitations.

3. Data Sharing and Third Parties

We do not sell or lease your personal information. We use third-party tools to handle our waiting list sign-ups (via Resend — resend.com). Your data is securely processed in compliance with standard security regulations.

4. Cookies

We use essential cookies to keep our website running and, if you consent, basic analytical cookies to help us understand site traffic and usage. You can manage your preferences at any time using our cookie consent banner.

5. Your Rights (GDPR)

Under the GDPR, you have the right to request access to the personal data we hold about you, request corrections, or request that we delete your email address from our waiting list. To do so, please contact us.
